How StreamPay works

1
Open. The sender locks USDC and picks a window (start → stop). The funds accrue to the recipient linearly, second by second.
2
Withdraw anytime. The recipient pulls whatever has streamed so far, as often as they like. No waiting for the end.
3
Cancel fairly. Either party can cancel: the recipient keeps everything streamed up to that instant, the sender is refunded the rest. The escrow stays exactly solvent at all times.

No owner, no admin keys, no protocol fee. Built for the Arc agentic economy: pay an AI agent per second of work, meter an API by usage, settle subscriptions continuously, in USDC (the chain's native gas asset) or EURC, switchable at the top.
